Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Health Issues

Q: How do I know if I need a health test?
A: If you're feeling unwell or have symptoms, consult with a doctor for advice on necessary tests.
Q: How can I track my test results? A: You can view your test results by logging into your account on our website.

Orders

Q: How can I place an order for a test?
A: Simply select a test from our available options and book a home collection via our website or app.
Q: How do I cancel or reschedule my order?
A: Contact our support team through chat or call us at the helpline for assistance with cancellations or rescheduling.

Doctor Consultations

Q: How do I schedule a doctor consultation?
A: You can schedule a consultation by visiting our website and choosing your preferred doctor and time slot.
Q: Are the consultations online or in-person?
A: We offer both online and in-person consultations, depending on the doctor's availability.
